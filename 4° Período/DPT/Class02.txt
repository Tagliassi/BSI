• DDL – Data Definition Language
    - Composed of Create, Alter, and Drop commands
    - Responsible for shaping the database

• DML – Data Manipulation Language
    - Composed of Select, Insert, Delete, and Update commands
    - Responsible for manipulating data by adding, modifying, deleting, and querying

• DCL – Data Control Language
    - Subset of DML, composed of Grant and Revoke commands
    - Responsible for controlling user access to data.

The data model is graphically represented by an Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD).
